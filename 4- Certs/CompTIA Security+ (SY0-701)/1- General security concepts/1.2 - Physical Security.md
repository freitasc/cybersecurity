## What is it?

- Channel people through a specific access point

## Additional Information

- #### Access control vestibules
	- All doors are normally unlocked
	- Opening one door causes others to lock
	- Closing all doors makes all unlocked again
	- ==One at a time, controlled groups==
- #### Fencing / Barricading
- #### Cameras / CCTV (Closed circuit television)
- #### Guards / Police / Soldiers
- #### Access Badge or Physical Identification
- #### Illuminate dark areas
- #### Sensors
	- Infrared
		- Detect infrared radiation, such as the heat emitted by the human body.
		- Ideal in detecting human intruders, even in complete darkness
		- Not ideal for environments with changing temperature.
	- Pressure
		- Designed to detect weight or pressure changes when stepped on. 
		- They would not be suitable for an open space where movement needs to be detected across a larger area.
	- Microwave
		- They emit microwave beams to create an invisible detection zone
		- Ideal for open environments with changing noise and temperature
	- Ultrasonic
		- Emit high-frequency sound waves to detect motion
		- Ideal for closed environments with changing noise and temperature
	- etc
