## What is it?

- It is the process of making something unclear, but not impossible to understand
- It is a way to hide info in plain sight
	- Store payment info without storing credit card number
## Additional Information

- #### Steganography
	- Its origin is a greek word: _steganographia_ 
		- _stegan√≥s_ means "covered"
		- _graphia_ means "writing"
	- It is the practice of representing information within another message or physical object, in such a manner that the presence of the concealed information would not be evident to an unsuspecting person's examination.
	- Images
		- Embed the message in the image itself
	- Network
		- Embede messages in TCP packets
	- Invisible watermarks
		- Yellow dots on paper discriminate which printer made it
	- Audio steganography
		- Interlace secret files within audio
		- Mr. Robot reference hehe
	- Video steganography
		- A video is a sequence of images
		- Image steganography can be used in large scale
	- Tokenization
		- Replace sensitive data with a non-sensitive placeholder
		- Used everywhere on credit card payment
		- Usually it is a one use token, if someone captures the token it is probably already useless
		- No need for encryption
	- Data masking
		- hide some of the original data
		- Protects PII
		- May only be hidden from view
