## What is it?

- Combination of principles, fundamentals of security
- Sometimes referenced as the AIC triad
# ==Confidentiality + Integrity + Availability== 

## Additional Information

#### Confidentiality: Certain information should only be known to certain people
- Encryption
- Access Controls
- 2FA or MFA

#### Integrity: Data is stored and transferred as intended
- Hashing
- Digital Signatures
- Certificates
- Non-repudiation

#### Avaibility: Information is accessible to authorized users
- Always at your fingertips
- Redundancy
- Fault Tolerance
- Patching
