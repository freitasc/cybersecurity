## What is it?

- If your network and infra is secure and the employees are trained in phishing attacks, there isnt much an attacker can do
- They wait and have the mountain come to them
- Also known as "poisoning the well"

## Additional Information

- Determine which websites, network or places the victim group uses and take hold of it
	- Hacking a local coffee or restaurant network that is used by employees of the target company
	- Hacking a industry-related site so you can infect the target company by lateral moving
	- Hacking a partner of the company and using their emails to attack the target company
	- INFECT ALL VISITORS! but keep the lookout for the specific target
	- Very common on APTs and government related hacking
