## What is it?

- Hardware devices can have native vulnerabilities
- Every device poses a certain risk
	- IoT is everywhere

## Additional Information

- Firmware
	- Software inside a hardware
	- OS of a hardware device
	- Vendors are the only ones that knows how it works and can update it
- End of Life (EOL)
	- Time where manufactures stop selling and supporting a technology
	- It means that it wont be updated anymore, so the vulnerabilities are going to stay as they are
	- It is time for you to get rid of it and switch for something else
	- It is the last step of the life cycle of a technology
- Legacy platforms
	- Devices and technology who have long ago stopped being supported
	- It is recommended that you get rid of it for something else
	- If it cant be removed or substituted, it needs to be continuously monitored and kept in check
