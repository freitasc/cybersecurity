## What is it?

- Since everyone uses operating systems, it is a very attractive place for a hacker to get hold of
- But... OSs are usually very complex, with millions of lines of code
## Additional Information

- OS maintainers usually update their OSs constantly
- You should always keep your OS updated, monthly and on-demand
- It's a race between attackers, developers and you updating your OS
- ... but usually updating a OS can break deployment, so you need to test it
- Be ready for a rollback, so always keep backups
