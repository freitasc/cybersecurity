## What is it?

- Supply chain involves all the suppliers, manufacturers, maintainers, costumers and consumers
- Attackers can infect the chain along the way
- One exploit can infect the entire chain
## Additional Information
- Service Providers
	- they often have internal services, an opportunity for attackers
	- can be a vector of an attack
	- "poison the well"
- Hardware Providers
	- Use a small number of trustworthy providers
	- Put on strict controls over policies and procedures
	- Write contracts that stipulate the consequences if they become a vector
- Network Traffic / Switches
	- A perfect visibility and pivot point
- Software providers
	- Digital signature must be checked and confirmed
	- Updates and patches must be regularly made
	- Open source is not immune
