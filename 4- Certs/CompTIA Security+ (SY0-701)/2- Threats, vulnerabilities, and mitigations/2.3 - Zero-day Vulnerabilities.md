## What is it?

- Zero-day is a vulnerability that hasn't been discovered and disclosed to the public
- Can be the result of intense research and are often made into secret by criminal groups or nation-states
## Additional Information

- The vendor has no idea it exists
- After found, fixes can take a long time until they are made
- It is by definition an arms race
