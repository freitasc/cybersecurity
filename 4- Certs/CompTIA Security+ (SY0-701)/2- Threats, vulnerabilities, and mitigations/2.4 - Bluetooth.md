Tags: [[Bluetooth]]

## What is it?

- It is a way of exchanging data without internet

## Additional Information

- Bluejacking
	- Bluejacking is the sending of unsolicited messages or files over Bluetooth
	- It is often used as a prank or to advertise to nearby people.
	- It is, most of times, benign as it cannot gain access by force
		- But it can be used to send unwanted messages or files
		- Can be a way of phishing someone
	- It needs some one to "open the door" for the attacker
- Bluesnarfing
	- Bluesnarfing is the unauthorized access of information through a Bluetooth connection
	- It gains access by force, by exploiting Bluetooth vulnerabilities
	- Can be done a lot of different ways
		- Zero-days
		- Exploiting a known vulnerability in an unpatched or outdated Bluetooth
		- Many others
	- It is always malign as it seeks to access personal data
