Tags: [[Exploitation]]

## What is it?

- There are ways to get into a well-secured system
	- Exploiting with malicious code
## Additional Information

- It comes in many formats
	- Executables, scripts, viruses, worms, trojans etc
- Protection comes from different sources
	- Anti-malware, firewall, updates, EDR/XDR, good habits etc

- Virus
	- Virus work like real life viruses
	- They replicate inside your machine, opening multiple instances of themselves
	- ==They are known for causing performance issues by running multiple instances==
	- They occupy memory and storage this way
- Worms
	- Worms aim to infect as many machines as possible
	- They replicate themselves and send the replicas through different means
	- ==They are known for being able to use other programs to propagate==
	- ==They can propagate through the network as well as discord, social medias and other== means
- Spyware
	- They infect your computer and/or browser and try to steal as much data as possible
	- ==They often install themselves on browser so they can subvert them==
	- ==They can often be seen as that annoying new task bar on your browser or by changing the main page or wallpaper==
- Rootkit
	- ==They try to get the highest privilage on a machine and then try to stay there==
	- ==Often they are invisible by different means of obfuscation==
	- ==Can run programs and routines without you ever knowing==
- Trojan
	- ==Like the trojan horse, they disguise themselves as a normal program or executable==
	- They can have different means of working and aim on different objetives
