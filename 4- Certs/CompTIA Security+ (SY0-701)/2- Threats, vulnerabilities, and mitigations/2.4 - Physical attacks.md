## Additional Information

- If you have physical control of a server, you have full control of a server
- Lock your doors, ask for IDs, give guns to your guards
- RFID clonning
	- A attacker can copy the RFID code of a company's employee's ID card
	- That way he can make a clone of that card and breach the company
	- Duplicators are super cheap
- HVAC (Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning)
	- Think of that scene in mr. robot
	- You can fry a server by destroying the coolers
	- You can destroy the fire suppression system and start a fire on the building
