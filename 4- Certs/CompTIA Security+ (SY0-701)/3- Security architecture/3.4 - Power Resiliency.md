## What is it?

- How much your power source can mitigate issues and recover from outages
## Additional Information

- Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS)
	- Short-term backup power
	- Blackouts, brownouts, surges
	- Comes in different types
		- Offline/Standby
		- Line-interactive
		- On-line/Double-conversion
	- Features
		- Auto shutdown, battery capacity, outlets, phone line suppression
- Generators
	- Long-term power backup
		- Fuel storage is required
	- Power an entire building
		- some power outlets may be marked as generator-powered
	- It may take a few minutes to get the generator up and running
