## What is it?

- The process of purchasing, selling or destroying is a multi-step process
## Additional Information

- To buy and keep something
	- Start with a request from the user
	- Negotiate with suppliers
	- Purchase, invoice and payment
	- Central Asset Tracking System
		- Used by different parts of the organization
		- Ownership
			- Associate a person with an asset
			- Useful for tracking
		- Classification
			- Type of asset
			- Hardware vs Software
		- Monitoring
			- Inventory every asset
			- Associate a support ticket with a device make and model
			- Enumeration
			- Add an asset tag
- To destroy or sell something
	- Media sanitization
		- System disposal or decommissioning
			- Completely remove data
			- No usable info remains
		- Different use cases
			- Clean a HD for future use
			- Permanently delete a single file
		- One-way trip
			- Once it's gone, it's really gone
			- No recovery with forensics
		- Reuse the storage media
		- Sell to someone else
		- Or Physically destroy it
			- Shredder / Pulverizer
				- Great for documents, may be harder with hardware
			- Drill / Hammer
				- Great for hardware, quick and easy but makes a mess
			- Electromagnetic (degaussing)
				- Remove the magnetic fields
				- Completely destroys anything inside a HD, does not work on modern SSDs
			- Incinerate
				- Burn everything to dust
				- It is the final solution for everything, but you need to pay the fuel
		- Destruction can be done by a 3rd party
			- They destroy it and send you a Certificate of Destruction
				- A paper trail of broken data
				- You know exactly what was done
