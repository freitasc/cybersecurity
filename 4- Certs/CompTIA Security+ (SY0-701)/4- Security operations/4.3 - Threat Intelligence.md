## Additional Information

- Data is everywhere
- Research the threats

- OSINT (Open-Source Intelligence)
	- Public available sources
	- Social media and forums is a good place to look
	- Hacker forums are great for this (its all on the dark web)
		- RaidForums, BreachForums, Nulled, RAMP are currently (jan 2026) seized by FBI
		- XSS forum is currently (jan 2026) still up and running
		- Dread is also fun, its like reddit but for nerds
	- Government data
	- Commercial data
	- Some companies sell OSINT as a service
	- Other companies make OSINT free and open
		- Cyber Threat Alliance (CTA)
			- Members upload specifically formatted threat intelligence
			- CTA scores each submission and validates across other submissions
			- Other members can extract the validated data
