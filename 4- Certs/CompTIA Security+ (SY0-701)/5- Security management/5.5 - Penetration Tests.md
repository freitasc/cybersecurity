Tags: [[Penetration Testing]]

## What is it?

- A simulated cyberattack on a system, network, or application to identify security vulnerabilities before they are exploited by malicious actors

## Additional Information

- Physical
	- Can you enter the building without a key?
	- Are there blind spots in the CCTV system?
	- Doors, windows, elevators etc
	- Against PCs
		-  Modify the boot process
		- Boot from other media
		- Modify or replace OS files

- Offensive = Read Team
- Defensive = Blue Team
- Integrate both for best results

- Working knowledge
	- Known environment (White box)
		- Full disclosure
	- Partially known environment (Grey box)
		- A mix of known and unknown
	- Unknown environment (Black box)
		- The pentester knows nothing
- Reconnaissance
	- Gathering a digital footprint
	- Understand the security posture
	- Minimize the attack area
	- Create a network map
	- Passive vs Active
		- Passive = Learning as much as you can from open sources (OSINT)
		- Active = Actively getting your hands dirty to get some intel
